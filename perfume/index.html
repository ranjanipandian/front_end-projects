SIGNATURE SCENT: Chanel No. 5 Overview: Founded in 1910 by Coco Chanel, Chanel is renowned for its timeless elegance and sophistication. Chanel No. 5, launched in 1921, remains one of the best-selling perfumes of all time.

Signature Scent: J'adore
Overview: Christian Dior launched his fashion house in 1946, with the perfume division following soon after. Dior perfumes are celebrated for their luxurious and exquisite compositions.

Signature Scent: Black Orchid
Overview: Tom Ford, founded in 2005, has made a significant impact on the fragrance industry with its bold, sensual scents. Black Orchid, launched in 2006, is known for its unique and intense aroma.

Signature Scent: Lime Basil & Mandarin Overview: Jo Malone London, established in 1994, is famous for its distinctive and elegant fragrances. The brand's unisex scents, such as Lime Basil & Mandarin, are celebrated for their fresh and sophisticated notes.
